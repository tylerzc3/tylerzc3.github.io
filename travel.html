Travel

I love to travel! I have been to a few different countries outside the US, including England and Costa Rica. I also seen quite a few states like Florida,the Carolinas, Montana, New york, and Virginia. I want to visit the west coast next to see California and Washington. My dream vacation would be to go to New Zealand and hike as many places as I could. I would also like to travel to dubai and see the tallest building in the world.

My favorite country to visit was Costa Rica. We went ziplining and some of them were almost half a mile long, over a 300 foot drop. It was an experience I will never forget. We also saw tons of unique wildlife like sloths, howler monkeys, coatis, and much more. We rented out a very nice house overlooking the rainforest. We hiked, drove, and swam all over the country.
